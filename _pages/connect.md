## Research Collaboration

<div class="collaboration-box">

I welcome collaborations on projects related to:

- **Health communication** and digital health interventions
- **Persuasive AI** and human-computer interaction
- **Social support** in online communities
- **Computational methods** for communication research
- **Mental health** stigma and medicalization

I am particularly interested in interdisciplinary projects that combine communication theory with computational methods. I collaborate with researchers from communication, public health, computer science, and psychology.

</div>

## For Students

I am happy to discuss research opportunities with students interested in:

- Health communication and digital health
- Computational communication research
- Human-AI interaction
- Social media and wellbeing

**Undergraduate students**: I occasionally supervise undergraduate research projects. Email me with your research interests and background.

**Graduate students**: I welcome conversations with prospective graduate students interested in similar research areas. Please include your CV and research interests when reaching out.

## Media Inquiries

For media inquiries related to my research on health communication, social media, or AI, please contact me via email. I am available for interviews and expert commentary on topics within my research expertise.

---

## Response Time

I typically respond to emails within 2-3 business days. During academic breaks and conference travel, responses may take longer. For urgent matters, please indicate "urgent" in your subject line.
