## Academic Resources

I share resources that have been helpful in my research and academic journey. These materials cover research methods, writing, and professional development. I hope these resources benefit other scholars in communication studies and related fields.

### Research Methods Resources

#### Computational Methods
- **Text Mining in R**: [Tidy Text Mining with R](https://www.tidytextmining.com/) by Julia Silge and David Robinson
- **Python for NLP**: [NLTK Book](https://www.nltk.org/book/) - Natural Language Processing with Python
- **Social Media Data**: [Social Media Lab Resources](https://socialmedialab.ca/) for social media research

#### Experimental Design
- **A/B Testing Guide**: Resources for online experimental design
- **Qualtrics Tutorials**: Official documentation for survey experiments
- **Effect Size Calculators**: Tools for power analysis and sample size estimation

#### Statistics & Data Analysis
- **R for Data Science**: [Free online book](https://r4ds.had.co.nz/) by Hadley Wickham
- **UCLA Stats Resources**: [Statistical consulting guides](https://stats.oarc.ucla.edu/)
- **Cross Validated**: Stack Exchange community for statistics questions

### Writing & Publication Resources

#### Academic Writing
- **They Say / I Say**: Academic writing templates and strategies
- **Writing Science**: Joshua Schimel's guide to scientific writing
- **APA Style Guide**: [Official APA resources](https://apastyle.apa.org/)

#### Journal Submission
- **Target Journals**: Communication journals accepting computational methods
  - *Journal of Computer-Mediated Communication*
  - *New Media & Society*
  - *Health Communication*
  - *Journal of Medical Internet Research*

#### Literature Management
- **Zotero**: Free reference management software
- **Connected Papers**: Visual tool for exploring literature connections
- **Research Rabbit**: AI-powered literature discovery

- **SPSS Tutorials**: Video guides for statistical analysis
